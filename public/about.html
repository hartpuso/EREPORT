About eReport

eReport is an employee complaint and report submission dashboard designed to provide a secure, supportive space for staff to raise workplace issues. It combines privacy, transparency, and ease-of-use to encourage constructive reporting.

Core Values

  • Confidentiality: Reports are handled with care to protect employee identity.
  • Accessibility: User-friendly design for all employees.
  • Accountability: Clear tracking and status updates on complaints.
  • Safety: Encourages a safe workplace through responsible reporting.

How it Works

Employees select a report type, provide a description, optionally attach evidence, and submit. Managers review and respond, while employees track status in the dashboard.
